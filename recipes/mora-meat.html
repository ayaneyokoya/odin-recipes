Mora Meat

Description

A large hunk of minced meat packaged inside of a pastry. When you split the pastry in two, minced meat spills out. The taste of meat grease is all but gone, and the baking process has brought out the sweetness of the pastry. Such pastries are usually stamped with the word "Mora," all the better to represent wealth and fortune.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pork belly
  • 10 cm scallion
  • 1 tbsp cooking wine
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 3 red chili peppers
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 star anise
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp chicken stock powder
  • 1 shishito green pepper
  • 1 english muffin

Steps:

  1. Cut the pork belly into 3cm cubes
  2. Prepare one plate with 4cm of scallion and 4 cloves of garlic.
  3. Prepare a second plate with 6cm of scallion, bay leaves, red chili peppers, 4 cloves of garlic, a cinnamon stick, and star anise.
  4. Fill a pot with water and throw your pork belly cubes into the pot.
  5. Now take your first plate with scallions and garlic and throw it into the pot as well, and top it off with your cooking wine. Boil for 30 minutes.
  6. Take the cubes of pork belly out after the 30 minutes and set aside.
  7. In another pot, pour in 1 tbsp of cooking oil and sugar. Mix well and cook until the sugar is completely melted.
  8. Toss in your pork belly into the pot, along with soy sauce, dark soy sauce, salt, and your second plate of spices and vegetables.
  9. Stir and fry everything in the pot and then fill the pot with enough water to cover the ingredients. Simmer for 40 minutes.
  10. Now add in chicken stock powder and let it continue simmering for around 10 minutes.
  11. In a separate pan, oil the pan with 1 tbsp of cooking oil and begin frying your english muffin until golden brown.
  12. While you're waiting, take out your pork from the pot, cut it up into small pieces, and mince 1 shishito green pepper.
  13. Slice your english muffin so it acts as a pocket, and begin stuffing it with your green pepper bits and pork! Enjoy!
