Sweet Dream

Description

A dessert made out of almond. It has a silky-smooth texture with a long-lasting aroma of almond. It's named tofu only because of its tofu-like shape. This version is Xiao's specialty. His dream is very simple, and it is merely a lovely dream.

Ingredients:

Almond Tofu

  • 40g almond powder
  • 400ml milk
  • 220ml water
  • 4g agar powder
  • 55g sugar

Matcha Jelly

  • 1g agar powder
  • 120ml water
  • 25g sugar
  • 10g matcha powder

Osmanthus Syrup

  • 10g osmanthus flowers
  • 200ml water
  • 150g sugar
  • 8g honey
  • 1-2g salt

Steps:

Almond Tofu

  1. Pour your milk into a bowl and add the almond powder little by little, mixing well.
  2. Pour water, agar, and sugar into a pot on low heat and stir until fully dissolved.
  3. Bring to a boil, and then turn off the heat.
  4. Add the milk and almond powder mixture into the pot and then reheat over low heat.
  5. Once everything is fully combined, pour the mixture into a heatproof container and carefully remove any foam from the top.
  6. Set aside.

Matcha Jelly

  1. The matcha jelly will follow the same steps as the Almond Tofu.
  2. Pour water, agar, and sugar into a pot on low heat and stir until fully dissolved.
  3. Bring to a boil, and then turn off the heat.
  4. Add the matcha powder into the pot and then reheat over low heat.
  5. Once everything is fully combined, pour the mixture into a heatproof container using a strain and carefully remove any foam from the top.
  6. Now put both the almond tofu and matcha jelly into the fridge and let it chill for about an hour.

Osmanthus Syrup

  1. Place your flowers into a colander and shake off the stems.
  2. Pour water and sugar into a pot on low heat and cook until the sugar is fully dissolved.
  3. Next, add your honey and salt into the mixture and mix well.
  4. Once everything has fully dissolved, turn off the heat and add the osmanthus flowers. Stir slowly for about two minutes and pour into a sterilized jar.

Assembly

  1. Now that all of the items are prepared, begin cutting the almond tofu into medium-size cubes, and the matcha jelly into tiny cubes.
  2. Place tofu on the plate, and stack one on top. Place the smaller matcha cubes on top of the almond tofu.
  3. Pour the osmanthus syrup over the tofu, and it is complete!
  4. Optional: Add white, pink, and green small mochi balls around the almond tofu and enjoy.
