Sweet Dream

Description

A dessert made out of almond. It has a silky-smooth texture with a long-lasting aroma of almond. It's named tofu only because of its tofu-like shape. This version is Xiao's specialty. His dream is very simple, and it is merely a lovely dream.

Ingredients:

Almond Tofu

  • 40g almond powder
  • 400ml milk
  • 220ml water
  • 4g agar powder
  • 55g sugar

Matcha Jelly

  • 1g agar powder
  • 120ml water
  • 25g sugar
  • 10g matcha powder

Osmanthus Syrup

  • 10g osmanthus flowers
  • 200ml water
  • 150g sugar
  • 8g honey
  • 1-2g salt
